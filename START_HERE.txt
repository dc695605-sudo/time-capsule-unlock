DIGITAL TIME CAPSULE

This little screen is sealed until its unlock date.

When you are ready to check it:

1. Plug the Arduino/time capsule into a computer with the USB cable.
2. Open unlock.html in Chrome or Edge.
3. Click "Connect to Time Capsule".
4. Choose the Arduino serial device if the browser asks.

The page sends the current computer time to the capsule.
If the unlock date has arrived, the screen opens and shows the photos.
If not, it stays sealed.

Do not remove the SD card to look for photos. The memories are meant to open
through the time capsule screen.
